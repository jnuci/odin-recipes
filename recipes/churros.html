Description

These churros will be sure to return you to those nostalgic days of walking around the swap-meet with you family as a child. Not all delights are good for you, but this recipe serves you up a nutritious and delicious dessert to be served alongside your favorite dinner plate.

Don't have much time on your hands? No problem! Soon enough you'll have enough cinnamon, sugar, and sweetness to forget all about your problems

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cups mafalda noodles
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/3 cup feta cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Preferred seasonings (PREFERRED DOES NOT MEAN OPTIONAL)

Steps

  1. Heat Skillet.
  2. Heat sauce and seasonings for approx. 15 minutes on low.
  3. Fill large pot of water and bring to boil.
  4. Cook noodles until slightly tender, about 8 minutes.
  5. Add drained noodles to heated sauce.
  6. Stir noodles until completely coated.
  7. Apply layers as you like.
  8. Broil until cheese bubbles, about 4 minutes
  9. That's it!
