Description

These churros will be sure to return you to those nostalgic days of walking around the swap-meet with you family as a child. Not all delights are good for you, but this recipe serves you up a nutritious and delicious dessert to be served alongside your favorite dinner plate.

Don't have much time on your hands? No problem! Soon enough you'll have enough cinnamon, sugar, and sweetness to forget all about your problems

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • granulated sugar
  • cinnamon
  • salt
  • all purpose-flour
  • 1 large egg
  • vanilla extract
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Whisk together sugar and cinnamon
  2. Heat oil in large pot
  3. Add water, butter, sugar, and salt in large saucepan
  4. Add flour and reduce heat. Stir until smooth
  5. Transfer to mixing bowl.
  6. Add vanilla and egg
  7. Squeeze mixture through nozzel into oil
  8. Let each fry for 2 minutes per side
  9. Coat in sugar and cinnamon mix
