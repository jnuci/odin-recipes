Protein Pasta Bake Recipe

Description

This dish is made with 3 types of cheese, combining the mouthwatering flavors into a dish that will not only satisfy your hunger but also hit your macros. There isn't much more we could pack into a meal this simple, and your whole family will love it.

The level of difficulty and quick preparation time also makes this lasagna perfect for college students looking to save time with quick calories.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cups mafalda noodles
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/3 cup feta cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Preferred seasonings (PREFERRED DOES NOT MEAN OPTIONAL)

Steps

  1. Heat Skillet.
  2. Heat sauce and seasonings for approx. 15 minutes on low.
  3. Fill large pot of water and bring to boil.
  4. Cook noodles until slightly tender, about 8 minutes.
  5. Add drained noodles to heated sauce.
  6. Stir noodles until completely coated.
  7. Apply layers as you like.
  8. Broil until cheese bubbles, about 4 minutes
  9. That's it!
