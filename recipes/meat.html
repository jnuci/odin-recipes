Description

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup seasoned breacrumbs
  • 3 tbsp. milk

Steps

  1. Cut the drumsticks into tulips by using using scissors to cut meat loose.
  2. Roll meat down to bone until inside out
  3. Soak 1/3 of breadcrumbs in milk
  4. Knead mix, then soak again.
  5. Fold meaty end around egg
  6. Cover drumstick in breadcrumb mixture
  7. Bake at 400 degrees Farenheit for 15-20 miinutes
